Stage 2 — Top 24

S2S Vote (÷bench×10) + 2nd Poll Vote (÷bench×10) added to Stage 1 carry-over

Stage 2 rules: All 24 stories compete. Enter S2S and Poll raw scores for every story.
Fast Track to Top 12: Top 2 S2S scorers + Popularity Vote winner advance automatically (S2S fast tracks skip Poll).
Promotion ranking uses S1 + S2S only — Poll score is shown for reference but does not count toward Top 12 promotion.
The remaining spots fill from the non-fast-tracked stories ranked by S1+S2S score.

Stage 3 — Top 12 · Head-to-Head

H2H Vote (out of 10, entered directly) added to S2 score → Top 8

Stage 3 formula: S2 Promo Score (S1+S2S) + H2H Vote weighted = S3 Total. Top 8 advance to the Final.
H2H scoring: Enter the raw H2H vote total for each story. The tool auto-converts to out of 10 using the highest raw score as the benchmark (same method as public voting).
