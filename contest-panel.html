Reviewer Instructions

Core Review Rules
📖
Evaluate First 3 Episodes Only
Regardless of total episode count, only the first three published episodes are evaluated. Stories with fewer than 3 episodes are ineligible — notify contest admin immediately.
⚖️
Full 0–5 Scale Required
Use the complete scoring range. A 5 represents excellence. A 3 represents average. A 0 means absent or severely deficient. Avoid defaulting to 3–4 for every criterion.
🔒
Strict Confidentiality
Do not share story content, scores, or reviewer deliberations outside official channels. Breach of confidentiality may result in disqualification from the review team.
🚫
Conflict of Interest
Do not evaluate a story you beta-read, edited, or authored. Do not review close friends or collaborators. Request reassignment immediately if a conflict exists.
🎯
Objectivity Over Preference
Your personal taste in genre, style, or author must never affect scoring. A genre you dislike can still be executed excellently. Score on rubric criteria alone.
⚠️
Content Violations
If you encounter plagiarism, explicit sexual content, hate speech, or glorification of harm — stop immediately, document, and notify contest admin. Do not independently disqualify.
