Author Details

Interview Scoring Rubric — 100 Points Total
Professionalism (PRO) — 30 pts
Is the tone respectful, composed, and appropriate for AOTY? Look for confidence, maturity, and awareness of what the competition represents.
Score guide:
25–30 · Highly professional, respectful, confident
19–24 · Professional with only minor slips
13–18 · Adequate tone but inconsistent
7–12 · Mostly unprofessional or inappropriate
0–6 · Careless or inappropriate throughout
Professionalism Score / 30
Response Composure (RC) — 15 pts
Are answers calm, structured, and focused? Well-composed responses address questions directly without rambling. Tricky questions handled with grace.
9–10 · Calm, focused, well-structured
7–8 · Mostly composed, minor rambling
5–6 · Somewhat structured but loses focus
3–4 · Disorganized, hard to follow
0–2 · Scattered or flustered
Response Composure Score / 15
Grammar & Spelling (G&S) — 20 pts
Grammar, spelling, sentence flow, and readability. Does not need to be formal, but should meet professional standards where errors don't distract.
17–20 · No noticeable errors, fluent
13–16 · Few minor errors, clear overall
9–12 · Noticeable errors, still readable
5–8 · Frequent errors affecting clarity
0–4 · Very poor, hard to read
Grammar & Spelling Score / 20
Response Strength (RS) — 15 pts
The substance and depth of each answer. Strong responses are clear, impactful, and demonstrate genuine thought. Shallow or vague answers score poorly.
9–10 · Strong, clear, and insightful
7–8 · Solid with minor gaps
5–6 · Adequate but surface-level
3–4 · Vague or lacking depth
0–2 · Weak or non-answers
Response Strength Score / 15
Overall Appeal (OA) — 20 pts
Overall impression left by the author's responses taken as a whole. Engaging, authentic answers that reflect personality and leave a memorable impression score highest.
17–20 · Engaging, authentic, memorable
13–16 · Decent appeal, somewhat memorable
9–12 · Passable but unremarkable
5–8 · Bland, generic responses
0–4 · Unengaging or forgettable
Overall Appeal Score / 20
