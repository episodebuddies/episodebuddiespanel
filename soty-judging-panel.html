Directing Grading Rubric

⚖ 20% of final SOTY score · 100 points total
🎬 Score each criterion from 0 to 5. Click any criterion name to expand the full scoring descriptor. Each sub-section totals 20 points (4 criteria × 5 pts). The section auto-totals as you score.
B.1 · Camera Work & Scene Composition20 pts
Dynamic zooms, scene framing, creative camera angles, and scene transitions.
Criterion543210
Dynamic Zooms & Pans
5 — Fluid, intentional, cinematic zooms and pans serving a narrative purpose.
4 — Mostly smooth with minor inconsistencies.
3 — Mechanical or generic rather than thoughtfully placed.
2 — Abrupt, repetitive, or unpolished movements.
1 — Barely any camera movement attempted.
0 — None, or movements disrupt the reading experience.
Scene Framing
5 — Characters consistently well-framed; background, foreground and placement create compelling scenes.
4 — Generally strong with occasional lapses in spatial awareness.
3 — Basic framing but characters often randomly placed.
2 — Poor use of space; characters awkwardly positioned or cut off.
1 — Framing appears accidental.
0 — No attention to framing.
Creative Camera Angles
5 — Varied, inventive angles enhancing storytelling deliberately.
4 — Good variety used confidently, elevating key scenes.
3 — Some variety but defaults to standard shots mostly.
2 — Limited or repetitive angles throughout.
1 — Almost entirely static or default angles.
0 — No variation; single default shot throughout.
Transitions Between Scenes
5 — Seamless, carefully chosen transitions matching each scene's tone.
4 — Mostly smooth with occasional jarring choices.
3 — Basic transitions, inconsistently applied.
2 — Abrupt or out-of-place transitions, scene changes are disorienting.
1 — Very few transitions; most scene changes are blunt cuts.
0 — No transitions or transitions break immersion.
